Foundation Well-Being Assessment

Sleep and Rest

1. How well do you sleep?

2. How often do you wake up during the night?

3. How rested do you feel in the morning?

4. How often do you have difficulty falling asleep?

5. How satisfied are you with the quality of your sleep?

Food and Drink

1. How often do you drink alcohol?

2. How often do you eat fruits and vegetables?

3. How mindful are you of portion sizes?

4. How often do you consume processed foods?

5. How much water do you drink a day?

Movement and Energy

1. How often do you engage in physical activity?

2. How do you incorporate movement into your daily routine?

3. How varied is your exercise routine?

4. Do you feel energised in the day?

5. How do you overcome barriers to physical activity?

Connection and Influence

1. How satisfied are you with your social life?

2. How often do you engage in meaningful conversations?

3. How well do you handle conflicts in relationships?

4. How comfortable are you in forming new connections?

5. How much value do you place what you think you should do for others?

Emotional Resilience

1. How often do you feel happy?

2. How well do you manage stress?

3. How easily do you experience emotional fluctuations?

4. How often do you experience moments of joy?

5. How in touch are you with your emotions?

Vulnerability and Responsibility

1. How comfortable are you with expressing your true feelings?

2. How often do you take responsibility for your actions?

3. How open are you to feedback and constructive criticism?

4. How well do you handle admitting mistakes?

5. How willing are you to share your vulnerabilities with others?

Security and Wealth

1. How confident are you in your financial planning for the future?

2. How satisfied are you with your current financial situation?

3. How well do you manage your expenses and budget?

4. How do you feel about your current level of savings?

5. How important is financial security to you?

Purpose and Self-Worth

1. Do you feel like you have a clear sense of purpose in life?

2. How often do you engage in activities that align with your personal values?

3. How satisfied are you with your current achievements and contributions?

4. How much do you rely on external validation for your self-worth?

5. How much do you prioritize self-care and personal growth in your life?

Environment and Belonging

1. How often do you spend time in nature or outdoor environments?

2. How connected do you feel to your local community and neighbours?

3. How well do you feel you belong in your current living environment?

4. How important is it for you to live in a place that aligns with your values?

5. How much do you actively contribute to the well-being of your living environment?

Self-Awareness

1. How well do you understand your own emotions?

2. How often do you engage in self-reflection?

3. How open are you to receiving feedback about yourself?

4. How aware are you of your strengths and weaknesses?

5. How well do you recognise patterns in your thoughts and behaviours?
